About Me:

Hello! My name, as you may have realized, is Kyros. I am a Queens, NYC native who is currently studying journalism at the University of Maryland.

My love for photography started in the 5th grade during spring break, when I went to take photos of wildlife with my uncle. He taught me the basics of his Canon t3 and sent me off to see what I could capture. It wasn't until freshman year of high school that I purchased my own camera in preperation for my trip to Greece in the summer. Ever since then, I have been in love with photography and I try to take my camera with me where ever I go.

Over the last couple of years I have shot several events ranging from prom photos to professional soccer matches. If you'd like to learn more about what I've done, check my clips page and resume page below.
