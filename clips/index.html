    Nikon Photo Contest

    The Powerful Poet

    Nikon just released their winning images for their 2020-21 photo contest (nikonphotocontest) and to say that I was absolutely shocked to see my photo chosen is an understatement. This photo was taken in Astoria Park during one of the Black Lives Matter marches last year. It was the first one I had gone to. After a small march, everyone gathered around to listen to individuals share their stories and thoughts with the crowd. • There’s plenty to be said on the matter, you can see some of my thoughts in previous posts and possibly more on the Nikon photo contest site later on. But overall, this image came about because of a movement to combat racial injustice in our country. It is important to remember that even though this photo was taken a year ago, and weekly marches have ended, the systemic issues remain.
